Major Central Schemes (India)

PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)
Income support of ₹6,000 per year (paid in installments) to eligible landholding farmer families.
Official site Search: How to apply
PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)
Crop insurance scheme that protects farmers against crop loss due to natural calamities, pests or diseases.
Official site Search: Claim process
Soil Health Card Scheme
Soil testing and nutrient status reports with fertilizer recommendations for each farmer holding.
Official site Search: Soil Health Card
Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
Short-term credit to farmers for cultivation, maintenance, and other needs at concessional interest rates.
Schemes portal Search: Apply KCC
e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)
Online trading platform to help farmers get better and transparent prices in regulated mandis.
Official site Search: Sell on eNAM
PMKSY (Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana)
Focus on expansion of irrigation, water use efficiency and "more crop per drop".
Official site Search: PMKSY details
MIDH (Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture)
Supports Horticulture production, post-harvest handling, and marketing through various interventions.
Horticulture portal Search: MIDH
National Food Security Mission (NFSM)
To increase production of rice, wheat, pulses and other crops through area expansion and productivity enhancement.
Official site Search: NFSM
NABARD Schemes & Refinance
Credit support, refinance and development programs for agriculture and rural development.
NABARD official Search: NABARD schemes
RKVY (Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana)
State-centric scheme to incentivize agriculture growth by funding approved state projects.
RKVY portal Search: RKVY
National Horticulture Board (NHB) Programs
Support for horticulture projects, nurseries, planting material, and post-harvest activities.
NHB official Search: NHB schemes
Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana (PM-KMY)
Pension scheme for small and marginal farmers on completion of 60 years of age (contribution based).
Search: PM-KMY
Agri Infrastructure Fund (AIF)
Credit support for investment in post-harvest management and value addition facilities.
Search: AIF
National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NMAET)
Strengthens extension services, capacity building and diffusion of agricultural technologies.
Search: NMAET
